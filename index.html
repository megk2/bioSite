A Bit About the BioSite

About Meredith

This BioSite is written about my sister, who inspires me daily. I look forward to introducing you to her here.

The Interview Text

1) How would those who know you describe you?

    “ultimately, you’d have to ask them, but I would hope that they would say that I am a good sister and daughter and friend. I know that Meg has said that I’m her favorite person. Being a twin is awesome.”

2) What do you do for work?
    “I teach middle school band. I love music and I love sharing that with the next generation and allowing kids who have very little outlet to express themselves through music. It was a great experience for me in band to be around so many friends and make music together, and I wanted to be a part of that as I grew up.”

3) What is your favorite memory?
    “I guess I have to say one of my favorite memories is adopting by dog George. I love spending each day with him and seeing him grow. He’s so energetic and animated, but even as he gets older he still looks like a little puppy. He really likes to play fetch, aunt tear up his toys, but it’s worth it.”

4) What do you like to do when you are not working?
    “I don’t have much free time outside of school, because I tend to run all of the afterschool rehearsals for the band. I also help with our schools drama club, who is putting on a musical right now and so it’s taking up a lot of my time. It is fun though. I guess when I’m not working I tend to like to enjoy my days off with a good cup of coffee, getting together with friends, or playing with George.”

5) If you could pick anywhere in the world to live that is not the US, where would it be?
    “probably the UK, how do you spell some time there and I really like the culture in the slower pace. The people there are also really nice and the weather is really good a lot of the time. It’s not too hot and not too cold.”

6) Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
    “I hope to be working and own a home and taking each day as it comes. I think its important to be present. Plan, but not so much that you are missing the current moment.”

7) Why did you choose to study music?
    “Play trolls music because I’ve always enjoyed it. I really like playing the flute and studying music as a whole. There’s so much more about music than just the sound. I guess I just like having the skill, and being able to see something awesome come out of it.”

8) What is the biggest difference between you and your sister?
    “I think I’m more reserved than Meg, Meg is really outgoing and likes to spend a whole lot of time with friends, I like hanging with friends too, but I need my time to myself.”

9) Who do you think you are most like?
    “ I think I’m most like Meg, because we just really understand each other even though we are different.”

10) If you were working in another field beside education, what would it be?
    “I’d probably work in the music business. I think it’s really interesting.”
